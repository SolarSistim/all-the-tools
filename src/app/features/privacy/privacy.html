Overview

At All The Tools, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our web-based utility tools.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data. All tools on this website run entirely in your browser. Any data you input into our tools is processed locally on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third-party services.

Browser Storage

Some tools may use your browser's local storage to save preferences or temporary data. This data remains on your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for essential website functionality, such as remembering your theme preference (light/dark mode). We do not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies.

Third-Party Services

Our website does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your browsing activity on our site is not monitored or shared with any external parties.

Security

Since all processing happens in your browser and we don't collect data, there is no data to secure on our end. However, we recommend using modern, updated browsers for the best security experience.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used our services, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our social media channels linked in the footer.
