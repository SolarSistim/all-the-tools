summarize The Bottom Line

This tip calculator provides two essential modes for dining and group situations: basic tip calculation and bill splitting. Calculate tips instantly by entering bill amount and tip percentage, with quick-select buttons for common percentages (10%, 15%, 18%, 20%, 25%, 30%, 35%) eliminating manual entry. View tip amount, total amount with tip included, and explanatory breakdown showing exact calculation ("Bill: $50.00 + 15% tip = $57.50"). For group dining, use the split bill calculator to divide total costs evenly—enter bill amount, tip percentage, and number of people to see tip per person and total per person with automatic calculation as you type. Perfect for restaurants, cafes, food delivery, rideshare services, taxi rides, hairdressers, spa services, hotel staff, tour guides, valet parking, or any tipping scenario. Quick tip buttons make selecting standard gratuity percentages effortless without calculator apps or mental math. Split bill feature ensures fair cost distribution when dining with friends, family, coworkers, or splitting expenses for group activities. Real-time calculation updates instantly as you modify any value—adjust tip percentage to see immediate impact on amounts, or change number of people to recalculate individual shares. All calculations happen locally in your browser with complete privacy—no bill amounts, tip percentages, or group sizes ever get transmitted to servers, ensuring confidential financial information stays on your device.
