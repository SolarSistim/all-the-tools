view_in_ar What is Blender 3D Software?

Blender is the world's leading free and open-source 3D modeling software. This powerful 3D creation software supports the entire 3D pipeline including modeling, rigging, animation, simulation, rendering, compositing, and motion tracking. As a complete 3D editing program, Blender has become the tool of choice for independent artists, studios, and hobbyists creating everything from feature films to video game assets. Many professionals consider Blender the best free alternative to expensive CAD software and commercial 3D design software suites.
